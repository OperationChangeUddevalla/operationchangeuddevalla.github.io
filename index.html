OperationChange Press Release

Information om OperationAid och OperationChange

More than five hundred days have passed now since the beginning of the war. Looking back it feels good to think of the real and lasting impact our efforts have had so far. OperationAid has a proven track record of providing aid when and where needed in a timely and effective way.

The situation on the ground evolves continuously and so must OperationAid. Growing larger and scaling up in order to be a part of the long term aid to Ukraine. Our future areas of focus being ambulances and acute medical care, rebuilding, mental health and the provision of acute humanitarian aid to civilians.

Starting this autumn we will be establishing educational programs and activities for women and children amongst the many Ukrainian refugees in Sweden.

These planned projects will be coordinated by our newly established Foundation.

OperationChange is a recently founded charity organization that will house the volunteer base that was previously with OperationAid. We will be focusing on three main areas. Relief-immediate humanitarian assistance. Resilience-meaning supporting recovery and endurance in underserved areas and, finally, Rebuild- long term sustainable development.

This enables us to help the Ukrainian people even more.

Should you have any inquiries, please feel free to reach out to respective spokesperson:

  • Ellen-Elena Reinolds, OperationAid Foundation, +46 70 604 40 32
  • Ludvig Ramestam, OperationChange, +46 76 097 80 13

The ongoing journey to alleviate as much as possible the suffering of the Ukrainian people is only possible due to the collective efforts of many. It feels great to be a part of that.

Ukraine still needs all the support they can get. Join us today in this important task.

Warm regards, Slava Ukraina!
